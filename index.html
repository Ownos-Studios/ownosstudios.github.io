About Ownos

Extrapolating from the above definition, we take the meaning of “Studios” very seriously. We believe that a true studio is a place where art is born and cultivated. A place where anyone with enough interest and talent can come in and create something new and incredible.

And just so, Ownos Studios is a comprehensive environment under which a number of projects, spanning across various industries, are undertaken and executed by the fresh talent of undergraduate students of India. We don’t believe that art is limited to a painter’s canvas or a musician’s instrument. We believe all creation to be artistic. And so, Ownos Studios aims to create an environment for all fields of work.
